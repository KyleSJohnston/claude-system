## Methodology Note

This analysis was produced by the Bazaar Competitive Analytical Marketplace:

1. **Diverse Ideation** — Five AI archetypes (Methodical, Contrarian, Pattern-Matcher, Edge-Case Hunter, Systems Thinker) independently generated scenarios
2. **Judicial Funding** — Four judge archetypes (Pragmatist, Visionary, Risk Manager, Quant) independently allocated research resources; funding was aggregated using weighted averages
3. **Obsessive Research** — Top-funded scenarios were investigated by domain obsessives (deep domain research) and search obsessives (live web signals via Perplexity)
4. **Analyst Translation** — Raw signals were synthesized into structured findings by the Analyst archetype
5. **Market-Proportional Report** — Word budgets were allocated proportionally to funding, with floor/ceiling constraints

*The methodology is designed to surface non-consensus views, stress-test assumptions, and produce actionable intelligence — not to confirm prior beliefs.*
